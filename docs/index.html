Energy Storage Units

  • On the market since 2017. We are pioneering the energy and mobility transition in Czechia.
  • First to put large Battery Energy Storage into operation in Czechia (2017, Solar Global).
  • Founding member of the Association of Energy Storage (2017, AKU-BAT).
  • Feasibility study of future energy self-sufficient Skoda Auto dealership network (first 4 pilot dealerships) (2018, SkoEnergo).
  • Electrifying Skoda Auto dealership network, implementing first own local consumption management, first dealer’s DC Fast Charger in operations (2019, Karireal Trinec).
  • Complete infrastructure delivery project of internal charging infrastructure for Prague transportation company (2018, 2019 DPP Prague).
  • Delivery of the first certified MOBILE Battery container in Czechia (2019, CE Energy).
  • Feasibility Study of charging infrastructure for first ever electromobility Skoda Product Training (CITIGOe iV, SUPERB iV) in Benidorm, Spain (2019, Skoda Auto).
  • Charging anywhere – we are delivering on our vision of providing energy anywhere – Charging infrastructure for Audi eTron VIP introduction and test-drives at Kacov Golf Course (2019, Audi).
  • Providing complete charging infrastructure at the biggest e-mobility event e-Salon (2019, ABF Letnany).
  • Safe and nimble cloud platform for aggregation of various local sources and/or consumptions with the aim to provide support for the new small and mid-sized energy market participants after EU legal Winter package implementation (2020, Nimble Energy).
